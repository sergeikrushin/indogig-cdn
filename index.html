Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get hired? +
After completing registration and verification (usually within 24 hours), you can start applying for shifts immediately. Many workers get their first shift within 1-2 days.
How do payments work? +
Workers receive weekly automatic payouts directly to their registered bank account. Businesses pay only for completed shifts with transparent commission rates.
What types of jobs are available? +
We offer shifts in hospitality (servers, bartenders, housekeepers), retail (cashiers, sales associates), logistics (warehouse staff, couriers), and events (event staff, coordinators).
Is Indogig available in my area? +
We're currently operating in Bali (Seminyak, Canggu, Ubud, Denpasar) and expanding to Jakarta, Surabaya, and other major Indonesian cities soon.
What documents do I need to register? +
Workers need a valid KTP (Indonesian ID card) and bank account details. Businesses need business registration documents and tax information.
How does the rating system work? +
Both workers and businesses can rate each other after shifts. High-rated workers get priority access to better-paying shifts and more opportunities.
